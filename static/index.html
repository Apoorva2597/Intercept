Evaluation Harness

Runs every scenario against its expected_routing_tag label. These labels were written by the project's builder based on a clinical background, not by a practicing nurse or physician reviewing these exact cases. This measures internal consistency, not validated clinical accuracy — that requires real clinician review of these scenarios, which hasn't happened yet.
